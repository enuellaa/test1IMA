A GUIDE TO BTS

BTS, which stands for BangTan Sonyeondan is a worldwide boyband from South Korea. It is the biggest band in the world.

Their fandom is known as "ARMY". The strongest fandom that ever existed.

This group was formed in 2010 and debuted in June, 2013 under the company, Big Hit Entertainment.
They are also known as; Bangtan Boys, Beyond the Scene, Bulletproof Boy Scouts and OT7
From 2013 to now they have grown to become a worldwide boyband, carrying the music industry on their shoulders and shocking the world. In 2020, BTS reached number one on the Billboard Global 200 and US Billboard Hot 100 with their song "Dynamite" which was nominated for the Grammy awards. Their new song in 2021, "Butter" was number one on Billboard for Nine weeks straight.
