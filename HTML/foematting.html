What is Motivation?

Scientists define motivation as your general willingness to do something.
It is the set of psychological forces that compel you to take action.
That's nice and all, but I think we can come up with a more useful definition of motivation.

So what is motivation, exactly? The author Steven Pressfield has a great line in his book,
The War of Art, which I think gets at the core of motivation.
To paraphrase Pressfield, At some point, the pain of not doing it becomes greater than the pain of doing it.
